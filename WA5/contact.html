Swarms

Swarming is a natural part of the honeybee reproductive cycle. If you see a swarm, please call your local swarm hotline to connect with a beekeeper who can collect it safely.

  • Boulder County Swarm Hotline: 1-844-779-2337 Ext. 3 or 970-213-3099 (Beth Conrey)
  • Denver Metro Swarm Hotline: 1-844-779-2337 Ext. 1
  • Northern Colorado Swarm Hotline: 1-844-779-2337 Ext. 6 (Berthoud, Fort Collins, Greeley, Loveland)
  • CSBA Swarm Hotline: 1-844-SPY-BEES

Boulder County calls go to Beth Conrey, who routes them by proximity. Expect calls from mid-April through June, with the busiest period from May 15–June 15.
