FAQs

There is a swarm of bees in my yard, what do I do?
Visit the Swarms page and call your area swarm hotline to find a beekeeper who can help you.

I'm a new beekeeper. How can I get involved in your community?
The best way to connect with BCBA beekeepers is to attend a meeting. New members are always welcome. You’re also invited to join the BCBA email discussion list and check out our discussion forum.

Where can I buy bees?
Bees are typically ordered in late winter or early spring for April delivery. You can connect with beekeepers organizing group orders at our February meeting or through our email discussion list.
